Health and Physical Education - Grade 6

This course introduces students to key aspects of personal health and wellness. Topics include nutrition, physical activity, mental and emotional health, and substance abuse prevention, empowering students to make healthy choices.

Physical Education 6 focuses on developing motor skills, knowledge of rules, and strategies for a variety of sports and physical activities. Emphasis is placed on teamwork, sportsmanship, and the importance of lifelong physical fitness.
