Health and Physical Education - Grade 11

This course focuses on advanced health topics, including family life and human sexuality, mental health awareness, and preparing for healthy independent living. Students will develop critical thinking skills to evaluate health information and services.

Physical Education III allows students to specialize in fitness and sports activities that align with their personal interests. The curriculum promotes leadership, advanced strategy, and the skills needed to maintain an active lifestyle beyond high school.
