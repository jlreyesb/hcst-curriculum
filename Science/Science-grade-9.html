Science - Grade 9

This foundational high school course provides a comprehensive study of living organisms and their vital processes. Key topics include cell structure and function, genetics, evolution, ecology, and human anatomy.

This advanced course is designed to provide a more in-depth introduction to biology, preparing students for AP Biology. It covers the same core topics as standard biology but with a greater emphasis on inquiry-based labs, data analysis, and critical thinking.
