Mathematics - Grade 9

Algebra 1 introduces students to the fundamental concepts of algebraic expressions, equations, and functions. Key topics include solving linear equations and inequalities, graphing linear functions, understanding systems of equations, and an introduction to quadratic functions and polynomials.

View Algebra I Curriculum

Geometry provides a comprehensive exploration of fundamental geometric concepts. Students will delve into Euclidean geometry, studying properties of points, lines, planes, and solids, with an emphasis on logical reasoning and proofs, transformations, congruence, and similarity.

This Math Proficiency course is designed to strengthen students' foundational mathematical skills and concepts essential for success in higher-level mathematics. The curriculum focuses on reinforcing arithmetic operations, number sense, and pre-algebraic concepts.

This Algebra 2 course expands upon the concepts introduced in Algebra 1, delving deeper into advanced topics like polynomial, rational, exponential, and logarithmic functions, as well as sequences, series, and probability.
